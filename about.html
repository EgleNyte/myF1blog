Welcome to my F1 blog

My name is Egle and this is my Formula 1 blog. I started watching Formula 1 13 years ago.
My dad is a huge fan, so because of him I watched my first race ever and felt in love with it.
Until this day, we both support Lewis Hamilton.

In the "Edits" section of this blog you can find the most beautiful moments of recent races, press conferences, trainings and everything Formula 1 through my eyes.
Pictures are uploaded as soon as new images from the photographers are available.
Please note that the images in "Edits" section are not taken by me, I only edited them. They are under the copyrights of their original owners at Getty images if not stated otherwise.
